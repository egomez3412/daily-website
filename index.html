About Us

Our Story

Green Thumb Nursery has been serving plant lovers and gardening enthusiasts for over 25 years. What began as a small family business has grown into one of the region's premier destinations for high-quality plants and expert gardening advice.

Our carefully curated collection includes everything from rare exotic plants to hardy natives, indoor houseplants, and landscape favorites. Each plant in our nursery has been personally selected and nurtured to ensure it thrives in your home or garden.

Beyond just selling plants, we see ourselves as educators and advocates for sustainable gardening practices. Our knowledgeable staff are always ready to help you find the perfect plants for your space and provide the guidance you need to help them flourish.
