About Us

Our Passion for Vinyl

Welcome to Vintage Vinyl, where analog meets artistry. Since 1978, we've been curating one of the most extensive collections of vinyl records in the city. Our passion for authentic sound and musical heritage drives everything we do.

Our store features thousands of carefully selected vinyl records spanning all genres and eras. From rare first pressings to modern reissues, we cater to collectors, enthusiasts, and anyone who appreciates the warm, rich sound that only vinyl can deliver.

Beyond just selling records, we're a community hub for music lovers. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you discover your next favorite album, and our regular listening sessions and local artist showcases create a space where music brings people together.
