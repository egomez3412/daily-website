About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Vinyl Vault, where the warm, authentic sound of vinyl records lives on. Since 1978, we've been a sanctuary for music lovers seeking the rich, unmatched quality that only vinyl can deliver.

Our carefully curated collection spans decades of musical genius - from rare first pressings to essential classics and emerging artists embracing the vinyl revival. Each record in our store has been personally selected for its sonic quality and cultural significance.

Beyond just selling records, we're a community hub for music enthusiasts. Our knowledgeable staff (who are passionate collectors themselves) are always ready to help you discover your next favorite album or find that elusive record you've been hunting for years.
