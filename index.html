About Us

Our Vinyl Journey

Welcome to Vintage Vinyl, where music lovers gather to rediscover the warm, authentic sound of vinyl records. For over 30 years, we've been curating an extensive collection of classic albums, rare finds, and new releases on vinyl.

What began as a small corner shop with a passionate collector has grown into a haven for audiophiles and music enthusiasts. We believe in the tangible connection vinyl creates between artists and listeners - a relationship that digital formats simply can't replicate.

Our knowledgeable staff shares your passion for music and is always ready to help you find that special album or recommend something new based on your taste. Whether you're just starting your vinyl journey or adding to a lifelong collection, Vintage Vinyl is your destination.
