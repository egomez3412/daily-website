About Us

Our Vinyl Journey

Welcome to Vintage Record Store, a haven for music lovers established in 1978. For over four decades, we've been curating an exceptional collection of vinyl records that span across various genres, eras, and cultures.

Our store houses thousands of vinyl records, from rare first pressings to remastered classics. We pride ourselves on our knowledge of music history and our commitment to preserving the authentic analog sound experience that only vinyl can deliver.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning your vinyl journey, our passionate team is here to guide you through our extensive collection and help you discover the magic of analog sound.
