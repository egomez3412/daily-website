About Us

Our Green Journey

Welcome to Green Thumb Nursery, a family-owned plant paradise established in 1998. For over two decades, we've been helping our community create beautiful, sustainable gardens and indoor plant havens.

Our nursery spans over five acres with thousands of plant varieties, from rare exotic specimens to hardy native plants. We pride ourselves on our sustainable growing practices and expert plant knowledge that we love sharing with our customers.

Whether you're an experienced gardener or just beginning your plant journey, our dedicated team is here to help you select the perfect plants and provide the guidance you need to help them thrive.
