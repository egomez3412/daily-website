About Us

Our Passion

Since 1978, Vintage Vinyl has been the premier destination for record collectors and music enthusiasts. Our carefully curated collection spans decades of musical history, from rare first pressings to modern limited editions.

We believe that vinyl records offer an experience that digital formats simply cannot match. From the warmth of the analog sound to the tactile pleasure of album artwork, vinyl connects listeners to music in a profound way.

Our knowledgeable staff shares your passion for music and is always ready to help you discover your next favorite album or track down that elusive record you've been hunting for years.
