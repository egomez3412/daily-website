About Us

Our Story

Vinyl Revival began as a passion project in 1985, founded by music enthusiasts determined to preserve the authentic sound and tangible experience of vinyl records in an increasingly digital world.

For over three decades, we've curated one of the finest collections of vintage vinyl in the country, specializing in rare pressings, first editions, and forgotten classics across all genres from jazz and blues to rock, soul, and beyond.
