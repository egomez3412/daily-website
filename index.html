About Us

Our Story

Established in 1978, Vintage Vinyl Haven has been a cornerstone of the local music scene for over four decades. What started as a small passion project in a garage has grown into the city's premier destination for vinyl enthusiasts.

Our collection spans genres and eras, from rare jazz pressings of the 1950s to modern indie exclusives. Each record in our store is personally curated by our team of music aficionados who live and breathe vinyl culture.

Beyond just selling records, we're committed to preserving the art of analog listening and building a community where music lovers can gather, share, and discover new sounds together.
