About Us

Our Story

Vintage Vinyl has been a cornerstone of the local music scene for over 30 years. What began as a small passion project in a garage has grown into the city's premier destination for vinyl enthusiasts and music collectors.

Our carefully curated collection spans decades of musical history, from rare first pressings to remastered classics and contemporary releases. Each record in our store has been personally selected for its quality, cultural significance, or unique sound characteristics.

Beyond just selling records, we see ourselves as custodians of musical heritage. Our knowledgeable staff are always ready to help you discover your next favorite album or find that elusive record you've been searching for years.
