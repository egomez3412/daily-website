About Us

Our Story

Vintage Vinyl was established in 1978 with a simple passion: to preserve and celebrate the timeless art of analog music.

For over four decades, we've been curating an exceptional collection of vinyl records spanning all genres and eras. From rare first pressings to remastered classics, our inventory is a treasure trove for collectors and music enthusiasts alike.

Our knowledgeable staff shares your passion for music and is always ready to help you discover your next favorite album or track down that elusive record you've been searching for. We believe in the warm, authentic sound that only vinyl can deliver.
