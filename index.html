About Us

Our Story

Vinyl Vault began as a passion project in 1984 by a group of audiophiles determined to preserve the authentic sound of analog music. What started in a small basement shop has grown into Chicago's most respected destination for vinyl enthusiasts.

Our collection spans decades of musical history, featuring everything from rare first pressings to essential albums across all genres. Each record is personally selected, cleaned, and graded to ensure the highest quality listening experience.

We believe vinyl offers a warmth and depth that digital formats simply can't replicate. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to help you discover your next favorite album or find that elusive record you've been searching for years.
